Crostini with Arugula and Walnuts Pesto

A super quick and easy recipe of appetizer. These are the perfect party food full of fresh and rich flavors. Make extra, freeze it, and add to your pasta when you need quick dinner. Enjoy!

Prep time: 15 min

Servings: 4 crostini

Course: appetizer

Ingredients:
  • 4 slices of French baguette
  • 1/2 cup of walnuts
  • 1 bunch of arugula
  • 50g of Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 6 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the pesto: add walnuts, arugula, garlic, salt, and olive oil to a food processor and combine on high for 1-2 minutes. Add 2/3 of grated Parmesan and combine for 1 more minute.
  2. Heat a little bit of oil in the pan. Toast each side of the baguette slices until light brown.
  3. Spread 1-2 tablespoons of pesto on top of each slice. Sprinkle with the leftover of Parmesan and arugula.
